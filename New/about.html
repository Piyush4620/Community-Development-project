We are trying to create a better world, secure against phishing

As the world has come closer and closer, it has certainly brought its share of problems. The digital age has introduced us to a new threat called ‘Cyber Attacks’. Our motive is

  • To keep the security experts updated with current trends in the field of cyber security.
  • To carry out essential campaigns for spreading awareness among society.
  • To focus more on spreading awareness and educating people about the threat.
  • Connecting the experts and the victims of the cyber attacks.

We plan to collaborate with some organisations, students, NGOs who could carry out those campaigns. If anyone falls victim to these attacks our website is available to help them out. We are open for the contribution of willing users. We need such active enthusiasts to disguise such hackers and engage them so that they could not approach any new victim in that mean time. And to know what techniques the phisher is using.
